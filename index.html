About J's Greens

When we say rare, we mean off-the-beaten-path, deeply flavorful heirloom vegetables. Grown in greenhouses and indoor grow rooms in central Philadelphia, we specialize in varieties you won’t find at grocery stores — crops chosen for their uniqueness and flavor and sought after by chefs.

Every order is harvested within 24 hours of delivery. Fresh, heirloom, and chef-inspired. Contact us now to find out what we are growing!

What do we grow?

All images shown feature crops grown by J’s Greens. We rotate our plantings monthly to offer variety and keep each harvest interesting for our customers. Each crop yields approximately 100–300 plants.

To date, we’ve grown borage, white strawberries(pine berries), red alexandria strawberries, flashy buttergem lettuce, sorrel (bloody dock), chervil, cardinal basil, thai basil, boquinho yellow peppers, black strawberry cherry tomatoes, and spoon tomatoes.

Want a taste?

If you’re interested in working with us, reach out and we’ll set up a time for a tasting. Crop availability varies. Free depending on availability!
